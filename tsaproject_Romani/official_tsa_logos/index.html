Welcome to TSA

The Technology Student Association (TSA) is a national organization of students engaged in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).













About Us


TSA is a national, non-profit organization of middle and high school student members who are engaged in STEM. Since TSA was chartered in 1978, almost 4,000,000 members have participated through competitions, intracurricular activities, leadership opportunities, and community service.

Our Mission


TSA enhances personal development, leadership, and career opportunities in STEM, whereby members apply and integrate these concepts through intracurricular activities, competitions, and related programs.
